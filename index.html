Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you provide?

+

I provide assets in multiple formats including FBX, OBJ, and Blender files. For game engines, I can deliver engine-specific files (Unreal .uasset, Unity prefabs) with optimized materials and LODs already set up.

How long does a typical project take?

+

Simple assets typically take 3-5 business days, while complex projects or asset packs can take 7-10 days. Rush orders are available for an additional fee with 1-2 day turnaround.

Do you offer revisions?

+

Yes, I include 2 rounds of revisions with every project. Additional revisions can be requested for a nominal fee. My goal is to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final result.

Can you work with existing assets?

+

Absolutely! I offer optimization services for existing photogrammetry assets, including retopology, UV unwrapping, texture optimization, and LOD generation to meet your performance requirements.

What about licensing and usage rights?

+

All custom work comes with full commercial licensing for your project. You'll own the rights to use the assets in your game and any related marketing materials. Specific licensing terms are outlined in our contract.
